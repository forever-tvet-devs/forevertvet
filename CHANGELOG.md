# Website Refinements & Updates

## Course Naming & Structure

- Updated all course names across the website to match the official school naming:
  - **Short Course Training:** Heavy Machinery Operation & Maintenance, Solar Technology, EV Cars
  - **3-Year Programmes (Level 3 to Level 5):** Computer Systems & Architecture, Electrical Technology, Land Surveying, Public Works
- Redesigned the **Our Courses** page with an alternating image-and-content layout for each course, making it easier for visitors to browse programmes visually
- Created individual **course detail pages** with full programme overview, subjects & modules, career pathways, lead instructor profile, and a photo gallery with lightbox preview

## Events

- Built a fully functional **Events page** with image-based event cards in a responsive grid layout
- Each event now features a banner image, date badge, category tag, and key details (time, location, public access)
- Created **event detail pages** with a large hero banner image, event description, and related events section
- Redesigned the **homepage Upcoming Events section** with image cards for a more engaging visual presentation

## Social Media Integration

- Linked social media accounts (Facebook, X/Twitter, Instagram) in the website footer with dedicated icons

## Application Form Improvements

- Extended the **year of completion range** from 1988 to the current year, making it accessible for all applicants regardless of when they completed their studies
- Updated the **date of birth selector** to support a wider range of years for easier selection
- Refined subject/combination fields to better reflect Rwanda's education system and accommodate international applicants

## Visual & Design Consistency

- Standardised **container widths** (`max-w-7xl`) across all pages for a consistent layout and padding
- Unified **colour scheme** across all programme cards, badges, and UI elements to use the brand's primary blue instead of varying per-department colours
- Ensured all **image galleries** across the site (courses, clubs, facilities) support **lightbox preview** with zoom, thumbnails, and navigation — matching the main Gallery page experience
- Updated **news detail pages** with a large hero banner image for a more immersive reading experience

## Leadership & Community Pages

- Redesigned the **Leadership page** with large, prominent portrait photos alongside names and titles
- Redesigned the **Headmaster's page** with a full-size portrait and the headmaster's personal message
- Redesigned the **Parents Corner** to match the headmaster page layout, featuring the Head Parent's portrait, message, and direct contact information

## Partners Page

- Redesigned the **Our Partners page** to display partner logos and names in a clean grid layout, with each partner linking to their respective website

## Clubs & Extracurriculars

- Redesigned the **Clubs page** with an alternating image-and-content layout matching the courses page design
- Created individual **club detail pages** with full overview, featured image, photo gallery with lightbox, and related clubs section

## Facilities

- Updated the **Facilities page** so all facility images support lightbox preview on click, allowing visitors to view photos in full screen

## Navigation & Routing

- Renamed "Departments & Subjects" to **"Our Courses"** across the entire website, including navigation, footer, and all internal links
- Updated route structure from `/academics/departments` to `/academics/courses` for clarity
